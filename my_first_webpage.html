All About Cats

What to Know about Cats!

What Cats can Eat:

Cats can eat a variety of foods such as fish, different meats, eggs, most fruits in small amounts, select types of vegetables, and very few grains.
Meats & Fish:
Different meats and types of fish are necessary for cats because they contain proteins and amino acids and this is the main source of nutrition for cats. Raw meats are not good for cats and they must be cooked for the cats to be able to safely eat them. Meats also need to be plain and have no seasonings and cannot be fried.
Fruits & Vegetables:
Cats can eat a variety of fruits like apples, bananas, pears, blueberries, and strawberries. And vegetables like lettuce, green beans, cucumbers, and carrots. Cats should only be fed fruits in small amounts and servings. Fruits contain a lot of sugar and can make you cat extremely overweight and obese or fat. Vegetables also give vitamins and minerals that are neccesary for a cat but should also be fed in small amounts.
Grains:
Cats can have grains like oatmeal and rice. Cats don't neccesarily need these carbs and should be fed in small amounts occasionaly.

What Cats can't Eat:

Cats can't eat a variety of foods in the fruit and vegetables category.
Fruits:
Cats cannot have grapes, oranges, cherries, and plums. These fruits can be very dangerous to cats and can cause symptoms like infections, obesity, and bladder issues. These fruits can cause seizures and even death./
