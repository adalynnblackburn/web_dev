What to Know about Cats!

The name for the color green originated from the Old English word "grene" which connects to the ideas of growth and grass. The color green has been used since the Ancient Egyptian times. They used something called malachite to make the color while Greeks used verdigris. The color green had a huge impact during the Impressionist movement and the emeral green color started growing its popuularity in fashion in the 1800s.

Cats can't eat a variety of foods in the fruit and vegetables category.
Fruits:
Cats cannot have grapes, oranges, cherries, and plums. These fruits can be very dangerous to cats and can cause symptoms like infections, obesity, and bladder issues. These fruits can cause seizures and even death./
Vegetables:
Cats cannot have onions and garlic in their diets. They can also have mushrooms but in small amounts and are not wild mushrooms. This can be fatal to your cat.
